About Us

A Meal that does its job

On a homestead in Georgia, our family began making burgers that would satisfy the ranch hands after a long day. Nearly 50 years later that same tradition is living on. After wrangling the workday, you too can work up quite the appetite. That's where Red Hat Ranch comes in. Our burgers are made for cowboys and cowgirls with a hankerin' for some mouthwatering chow. So gather the gang around and fire up the skillet. Then enjoy a delicious meal that does it's job, satisfying your hunger! Red Hat Ranch burgers are sold across the country and can be found in your grocer’s freezer. They are meant to be cooked when frozen and take only 10-12 minutes from your freezer to your table. You can grill them, broil them, bake them or throw them in the skillet. In three unique and delicious flavors, everyone will find a favorite.
